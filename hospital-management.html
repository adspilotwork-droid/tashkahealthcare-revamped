FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI chatbot work?

Trained on your hospital's documents and EMR context (where permitted) to answer staff queries, surface protocols, and assist coding. Access is role-based and every response is auditable.

Do you support full customization?

Yes. Unlimited customization during and after implementation — forms, workflows, templates, and reports. No features locked post-go-live.

What about data migration and configuration?

We migrate from legacy systems and configure modules, roles, templates, and reports, with validation and sign-off at each stage before go-live.

Is the platform modular?

Yes. Enable OPD, IPD, Pharmacy, Lab, Patient App, VRS, and AI modules individually or together — in any combination that suits your hospital.

What security and compliance standards do you meet?

Encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access, audit logs, data residency options. HIPAA-compliant, NABH-ready, SOC 2 Type II, HITRUST certified.

What integrations are supported?

LIMS, PACS, payment gateways, ERP/Tally, SMS/WhatsApp notifications, and 150+ EMR/HIS systems via FHIR/HL7-compliant APIs.
