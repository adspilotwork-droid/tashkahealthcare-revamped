FAQ

Questions From Hospital Administrators & Decision-Makers

What CEOs, COOs, Finance Heads, and IT teams ask before going live with Tashka.

How does the AI chatbot help hospital staff specifically?

Tashka's AI chatbot is trained on your hospital's documents, SOPs, and operational data. Staff ask it questions in plain language — "What beds are available in Ward 3?", "What's the billing status for patient Suresh in IPD?", "What's the SOP for pre-authorisation for United Health?" — and get instant answers without logging into multiple systems. Access is role-based: nursing staff, billing teams, and administrators each see the context relevant to their work.

Can we start with just OPD and add IPD later?

Yes. The platform is fully modular. Most hospitals start with OPD and pharmacy — highest volume, fastest to show ROI — then add IPD, lab, billing, and AI modules as each department is ready. You're never forced to go live with everything at once.

How does data migration from our existing system work?

Tashka's implementation team handles migration from your existing HIS, billing software, or paper records. We map data fields, run test migrations with sample data, and do full validation before cutover. Every migration has sign-off checkpoints — nothing goes live until your team is satisfied.

Can Tashka handle TPA and insurance billing?

Yes. TPA billing, corporate billing, government scheme patients (Ayushman Bharat, CGHS, etc.), and insurance claims are all handled — including pre-authorisation requests, claim submission, settlement tracking, and receivables management with aging reports.

Is the platform modular — or do we have to take the full package?

Fully modular. OPD, IPD, Pharmacy, Lab, Patient App, VRS, and AI can be enabled individually or in any combination. Pricing is scoped per module. You never pay for what you don't use.

How does multi-location management work?

All locations share one centralized platform. Pharmacy inventory is unified across branches. Patient records are accessible from any location (with permissions). Management gets consolidated dashboards across all campuses. Soukhyada Hospital manages 6 Karnataka locations this way.
