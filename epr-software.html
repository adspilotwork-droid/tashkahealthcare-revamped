Understanding EPR

What Is EPR Software?

Most Indian hospitals run disconnected systems: separate HIS for admin, different tool for pharmacy, paper nursing notes, Excel reports. Tashka EPR ends that fragmentation.

An EPR (Electronic Patient Record) is a unified digital record of every clinical interaction — from first OPD registration through discharge, across every department — connecting clinical data, billing, pharmacy, diagnostics, and operations in one longitudinal patient record.

FAQ

Common Questions from Hospital Decision-Makers

What CMOs, CFOs, and IT heads ask before going live with Tashka EPR.

What is EPR software and how is it different from HIMS or EMR?

EPR captures every clinical interaction from OPD registration through discharge across all departments. An EMR covers physician documentation only. HIMS adds the operational layer — billing, administration, supply chain. Tashka EPR combines all three: clinical records connect directly with billing, pharmacy, lab, and AI in one system.

Is Tashka EPR NABH-compliant?

Yes. Pre-configured NABH-compliant discharge summaries, consent forms, nursing notes, medication administration records, and care pathway templates ship with the platform. CSI and Soukhyada hospitals run full NABH documentation workflows on Tashka in production.

How long does implementation take?

A single-hospital deployment (OPD + IPD + Pharmacy + Lab) goes live in 4–12 weeks. Multi-site deployments use a phased rollout where each site signs off before the next goes live.

Can Tashka EPR integrate with our existing lab analyzers and PACS?

Yes. FHIR/HL7-compliant APIs and direct connectors integrate with LIMS, PACS/RIS, payment gateways, ERP/Tally, and SMS/WhatsApp. Integration planning is included in the Discovery phase at no additional cost.

How does the AI chatbot work?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) — answers only from your hospital's approved data sources. Never trains on your patient data externally. Every response is role-gated and audit-logged.

Do you support multi-location hospital groups?

Yes. Soukhyada Hospital runs across 6 Karnataka locations on a single Tashka platform with centralized management and unified reporting. Multi-site is standard, not a premium add-on.

What does pricing look like?

Modular pricing — you select the modules you need and pricing is scoped after a discovery call based on size, modules, and data migration complexity. No hidden fees post-implementation.

Cloud-based or on-premise?

Both. Tashka supports cloud, hybrid, and on-premise deployment. We recommend based on your data sovereignty requirements and infrastructure during the Discovery phase.
